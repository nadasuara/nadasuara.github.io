tidak semua kalimat bisa diubah menjadi kalimat pasif

because there are sentences which are using intransitive verb

sebab ada kalimat2 yg memakai kata kerja intransitive

Simple Future Tense

- You will be the prince ---- cannot

- I will be the princess ----- cannot

- We will make it out of this mess ----- the mess will be made out by us

- I will walk down the aisle ----- cannot

- I will say ----- cannot

Future Continuous Tense

- I will be waiting ----cannot

- I will be thanking my dad ---- my dad will be thanked by me
