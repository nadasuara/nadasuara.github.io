17. Tuliskan kata kerja verb1 verb2 dan verb3 beserta terjemahannya remember,look,attack,want,hear,try,leave,curse


Jawaban:

V1 , V2, V3 :

Remember , Remembered , Remembered = ingat , diingat

Look , Looked, Looked = melihat  

Attack , Attacked , Attacked = menyerang , terserang

Want , Wanted , Wanted = ingin

Hear , Heard , Heard = mendengar

Try , Tried , Tried = mencoba

Leave , Left , Left = meninggalkan

Curse , Cursed , Cursed = kutukan , terkutuk

Penjelasan:

Untuk terjemahannya, yg pertama itu verb 1 trus yg kedua itu verb 2 sama 3.
