26. 5.10-5 =A. Ten times five equal fiveB. Ten divided by five equal fiveC. Ten and five equal fiveD. Ten less five equal fiveTolong sekarang yaa di jawab nya mau di kumpulin hati ini juga​


Jawaban:

D. Ten less five equal five

ANSWER

10 - 5 = 5

D. Ten less five equal five

Explanation

The word "less" is a sign of " - " or minus.

The number " 10 " in the words are " ten "

The number " 5 " in the words are " five "

The sign " = " in the words are " equal "
