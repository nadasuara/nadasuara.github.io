16. Description agnes mo


Agnes Monica Muljoto (born 1 July 1986) is an Indonesian pop singer, songwriter, actress, dancer. She began her career in singing at the age of six. In addition to singing, she also became a presenter on a children's television show. When she was a teenager, Agnes started working as an actor. Her appearance on the soap opera Pernikahan Dini (2001) made her famous. Agnes then starred in a series of soap operas that made her the highest-paid teenage artist in Indonesia at that time.

39. Answer the world problem question A bottle of coke contains 2 litres.Jeevan pours 300cm3 into a cup.How much coke is left in the bottle in cubic centimeters?


Jawaban:

If a bottle of coke contains 2 liters, which is equivalent to 2000 cubic centimeters (since 1 liter = 1000 cubic centimeters), and Jeevan pours 300 cm3 into a cup, then the amount of coke left in the bottle can be calculated by subtracting the amount poured into the cup from the initial amount in the bottle:

2000 cm3 (initial amount) - 300 cm3 (poured into the cup) = 1700 cm3

So, there are 1700 cubic centimeters of coke left in the bottle

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

A bottle of coke contains 2 litres, which is equivalent to 2000 cm^3 (since 1 liter is equal to 1000 cm^3).

Jeevan pours 300 cm^3 into a cup. To find out how much coke is left in the bottle, we need to subtract 300 cm^3 from the initial volume of the bottle.

Initial volume of the bottle: 2000 cm^3

Amount poured into the cup: 300 cm^3

Coke left in the bottle: Initial volume - Amount poured

Coke left in the bottle: 2000 cm^3 - 300 cm^3

Coke left in the bottle: 1700 cm^3

Therefore, there is 1700 cubic centimeters of coke left in the bottle.
