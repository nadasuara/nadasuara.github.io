24. 1. Honda motorcycles... in Japan.A. is madeB. are makingC. are made D. is makes​


Jawaban: C. are made

Penjelasan:

“are” digunakan untuk beberapa kata ganti, yaitu: kata ganti orang kedua tunggal (you/kamu), kata ganti orang pertama jamak (we/kami), dan kata ganti orang ketiga jamak (they/mereka).

kata "are" mengacu pada motorcycles (motor dalam jamak)

C. Are made
Because there is a plural subject (motorcycles), so you have to use “to be” for plural subject, which is “are” is the correct choice. Then, beacuse the actual meaning of the sentences is “motor-motor honda itu dibuat di Jepang” then the most appropriate answer is “are made”.
