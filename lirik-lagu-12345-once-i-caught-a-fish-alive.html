6. Kalimat yang benar dari my father and i go fishing yesterday. he caught some fish but i only got one adalah


Jawaban:

Kalimat yang benar dari my father and i go fishing yesterday. he caught some fish but i only got one adalah

My Father and I went fishing yesterday. He caught some fish but I only got one.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan menggunakan bentuk kalimat Simple Past Tense karena menjelaskan aktivitas kegiatan di masa lampau.

SImple Past Tense:

(+) Subject + Verb-2 + Object + Time Signal (yesterday)

Semoga membantu ya.

Jawaban:

My father and i went fishing yesterday. He caught some fish but i only got one.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat tersebut adalah simple past tense karena terjadi di masa lampau (yesterday).

Jadi perbaikan kalimat yang benar adalah My father and i went fishing yesterday. He caught some fish but i only got one.

Simple past tense:

S + V2 + Object

Contoh:

Nana went to the mall last week

14. Cats A, B and C were catching fish at the river. Cat B caught 4 more fish than Cat C. Cat A caught 3 times as many fish as Cat B. The three cats caught 46 fish in total. How many fish did Cat B catch?


Jawaban:

Let's assume the number of fish caught by Cat C is x.

According to the given information:

- Cat B caught 4 more fish than Cat C, so Cat B caught (x + 4) fish.

- Cat A caught 3 times as many fish as Cat B, so Cat A caught 3 * (x + 4) fish.

The total number of fish caught by the three cats is 46, so we can write the equation:

x + (x + 4) + 3 * (x + 4) = 46

Simplifying the equation:

x + x + 4 + 3x + 12 = 46

5x + 16 = 46

5x = 46 - 16

5x = 30

x = 30 / 5

x = 6

Therefore, Cat B caught x + 4 fish:

Cat B caught 6 + 4 = 10 fish.

So, Cat B caught 10 fish.
