5. A piano cost $800 more in 2016 than in 2015. This was a 20% increase from the price in 2015. The price of the piano increased by 25% from 2016 to 2017. Find the cost of the piano a) in 2016 b) in 2017


The cost of the piano in 2016 was $4,800.The cost of tue piano in 2017 was $6,000.

In our daily life, we use percentages especially when it comes to money such as bank interest, discounts, salary increases, price increases for goods and so on. Percentage is a fraction that states the proportion of a number to the number 100 as a whole. It means, a fraction can be used as a percentage when the denominator is 100. A% of a number P is representing A/100 × P.

For more details, see the discussion of the following problems.

PEMBAHASAN :

• A piano cost $800 more in 2016 than in 2015. This was a 20% increase from the price in 2015.

If the pr of a piano in 2015 wss assumed to be P, then the right mathematical sentence to state the cost of the piano in 2016 is :

P + 20%P

or

P + $800

it means, 20%P = $800

P = $800 ÷ 20%

P = $800 × [100/20]

P = the cost of the piano in 2015 = $4,000

So, the cost of the piano in 2016 was $4,000 + $800 = $4,800.

• The price of the piano increased by 25% from 2016 to 2017. Then, the right mathematical sentence to state the cost of the piano in 2017 is : $4,800 + [25% × $4,800]

= $4,800 + $1,200

= $6,000

So, the cost of the piano in 2017 was $6,000.

18. gambarkan chord (dengan piano) chord: G#, C#m, Baug dan F#dim​


Jawaban:

Berikut adalah deskripsi chord yang Anda minta, dengan menggunakan notasi piano:

Chord G# (G# Major):

- G# (Gunung #) pada oktaf 4 (telunjuk tangan kanan)

- C (Do) pada oktaf 5 (jari tengah tangan kanan)

- D# (Rekang #) pada oktaf 5 (jari kelingking tangan kanan)

Chord C#m (C# Minor):

- C# (Do #) pada oktaf 4 (telunjuk tangan kanan)

- E# (Mi #) atau F (Fa) pada oktaf 4 (jari tengah tangan kanan)

- G# (Gunung #) pada oktaf 4 (jari kelingking tangan kanan)

Chord Baug (B Augmented):

- B (Si) pada oktaf 4 (telunjuk tangan kanan)

- D# (Rekang #) pada oktaf 4 (jari tengah tangan kanan)

- F## atau G (Sol # atau La) pada oktaf 4 (jari kelingking tangan kanan)

Chord F#dim (F# Diminished):

- F# (Fa #) pada oktaf 4 (telunjuk tangan kanan)

- A (La) pada oktaf 4 (jari tengah tangan kanan)

- C (Do) pada oktaf 4 (jari kelingking tangan kanan)

Harap diingat bahwa notasi chord yang disajikan di atas adalah untuk piano dan menggunakan notasi "sharp" (#) untuk menunjukkan nada yang ditinggikan setengah langkah.

Kunci C, yaitu kunci yang digunakan saat adanya 2 senar (1st) , 4 senar (2rd), dan 5 senar (3th) (fret)Kunci D, yaitu kunci yang digunakan saat adanya 3 senar (1st), 1 senar (2rd), dan 2 senar (3th) (fret)Kunci E, yaitu kunci yang digunakan saat adanya 3 senar (1st), 5 (2rd), 4 (3th).
