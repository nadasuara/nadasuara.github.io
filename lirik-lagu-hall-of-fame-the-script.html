5. The ratio of the number of boys to the number of girls in a hall was 3:5 after 84 girls left the hall,the ratio of the number of boys to the numbet of gitls became 2:1 how many girls were in the hall at first


Jawab:

There were 120 girls in the hall at first.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Boys : Girls = 3:5 ⇒ Boys = 3/5 Girls

Boys : Girls - 84 = 2:1 ⇒ Boys = (Girls - 84) × 2

(2 - 3/5) Girls = 168

Girls = 120

There were 420 girls in the hall at first.

If you want to check:

there were 120 girls, therefore there were 72 boys.

after 84 girls left the hall, there were 120 - 84 = 36 girls. Therefore the ratio become 72:36 = 2:1.

explore the art of lyric writing through ten complete lessons. Aligned with Common Core Standards, this

interdisciplinary unit also can be tied to math, music, social studies, and visual arts.

to a variety of learning styles and levels. Whenever possible, students should be encouraged to try new ideas

The Words & Music unit is composed of ten 45-minute lessons. The lessons are cumulative, each one

building on the next, so none should be skipped. The final project, writing a polished set of lyrics, combines

everything students have learned in previous lessons. Final lyrics are sent to the museum to share with

a professional songwriter, who will select a few works to set to music. Ultimately, students will hear the

songwriter perform some lyrics as finished songs.

Though lyric writing is short-form creative writing, it requires no less ability than any other kind of

creative writing. Of course, it also is just part of the task of songwriting, along with musical composition.

Students will gain a firsthand knowledge of the skill necessary to craft art that is memorable, evocative, and

appealing—a skill that, in the hands of a seasoned lyricist, can create great music.

23. The ratio of the number of boys to the number of girls in a hall was 3:5 after 84 girls left the hall,the ratio of the number of boys to the numbet of gitls became 2:1 how many girls were in the hall at first


Jawab:

There were 120 girls in the hall at first.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Boys : Girls = 3:5 ⇒ Boys = 3/5 Girls

Boys : Girls - 84 = 2:1 ⇒ Boys = (Girls - 84) × 2

(2 - 3/5) Girls = 168

Girls = 120

There were 420 girls in the hall at first.

If you want to check:

there were 120 girls, therefore there were 72 boys.

after 84 girls left the hall, there were 120 - 84 = 36 girls. Therefore the ratio become 72:36 = 2:1.

27. The perimeter of rectangular hall is 72 m. The length of the hall is 3 times its breadth. Find the length of the hall.


length = 3*breadth
perimeter = 2(length + breadth)
72 =2(3*breadth + breadth)
36 = 4 breadth
breadth = 9m

length = 3*9 = 27 m

Masuk dalam Rock and Roll Hall of Fame menjadi salah satu pencapaian tersendiri bagi seorang musisi. Meski disebut sebagai salah satu penghargaan tertinggi bagi insan musik, tetap saja bisa menimbulkan kontroversi.

The Beatles mendapat kehormatan untuk masuk dalam Rock and Roll Hall of Fame pada 1988. Penghargaan tersebut diberikan bertepatan dengan 8 tahun setelah wafatnya John Lennon.

Alhasil, tiga personel tersisa The Beatles yaitu Geogre Harrison, Ringo Star dan Paul McCartney pada saat itu diberi kesempatan untuk 'menginjakkan kaki' mereka di Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.

Tapi, Paul McCartney ternyata menolak datang dalam acara seremoni tersebut. Paul beralasan bahwa para personel The Beatles tak mungkin disatukan lagi walaupun untuk 'sekadar' menerima Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.
