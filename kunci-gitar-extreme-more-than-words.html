5. Writing assignmen about "Endangered Species" More than 200 words


Jawaban:

Orangutan

The orangutans (also spelled orang-utan, orangutang, or orang-utang) are three extant species of great apes native to Indonesia and Malaysia. Orangutans are currently found in the rainforests of Borneo and Sumatra, but during the Pleistocene they ranged throughout Southeast Asia and South China. Classified in the genus Pongo, orangutans were originally considered to be one species. From 1996, they were divided into two species: the Bornean orangutan (P. pygmaeus, with three subspecies) and the Sumatran orangutan (P. abelii). In November 2017, it was reported that a third species had been identified: the Tapanuli orangutan (P. tapanuliensis).

Orangutans are among the most intelligent primates; they use a variety of sophisticated tools and construct elaborate sleeping nests each night from branches and foliage. The apes have been extensively studied for their learning abilities. There may even be distinctive cultures within populations. Field studies of the apes were pioneered by primatologist Birutė Galdikas. All three orangutan species are considered to be critically endangered. Human activities have caused severe declines in populations and ranges. Threats to wild orangutan populations include poaching, habitat destruction as a result of palm oil cultivation, and the illegal pet trade. Several conservation and rehabilitation organisations are dedicated to the survival of orangutans in the wild.

Answer (Jawaban):

(Tugasnya lebih sulit dari sebelumnya)

Explanation (Penjelasan):

"The task is more difficult than before."

Kalimat diatas menggunakan rumus Comparative Degree.

~COMPARATIVE DEGREE~

Kegunaan (Usage):

Comparative degree digunakan pada saat kita ingin menyatakan ada 2 obyek yang berbeda (baik dalam bentuk kualitas maupun tingkatan) dalam sesuatu hal.

Comparative degree memiliki 2 pola sbb:

1. Adjective + /-er/ + than

Penjelasan:

/-er/ adalah akhiran (suffix) yang ditaruh tepat dibelakang kata sifatnya.

Contoh:

small + /-er/ => smaller (lebih kecil),

Contoh dalam bentuk kalimat:

The box is smaller than this one.

(Kotak itu lebih kecil daripada yang ini).

2. More + adjective + than

Contoh:

more + beautiful => more beautiful (lebih indah/cantik)

Contoh dalam bentuk kalimat:

Rina is more beautiful than Dewi. (Rina lebih cantik daripada Dewi)

Catatan:

(i). Gunakan pola adjective + /-er/ + than jika suku kata dari kata sifat (adjective) tersebut kurang dari 2 suku kata.

(ii) Gunakan pola more + adjective + than jika suku kata lebih dari 2 atau sama dengan 2 suku kata.

(iii). Bila adverb of manner digunakan dalam pola comparative degree ini maka, gunakan pola more + adjective + than

Contoh:

Shinta writes more quickly than Rudi.

23. Mana yang benar, more tall than me atau more taller than me atau taller than me


Jawabannya => More taller than me dan Taller than

Alasannya =>

More tall than me itu kurang tepat karena setiap kata adjective itu harus ditambahkan er kalau dibelakang ada "More".

Kalau kata sifatnya dalam comperative dan superlative tergantung dari jumlah suku kata dalam membandingkan suatu benda.

Kalau biasanya -er setelah kata sifat digunakan untuk kata sifat yang mempunyai satu suku kata untuk prefix -more sebelum kata sifat , digunakan jika kata sifat tersebut mempunyai more than ( lebih dari satu suku kata ).

Taller than itu juga boleh digunakan karena juga termasuk comparison/comperative yaitu kita membandingkan benda yang satu atau benda yang lain.Misalnya saya kasih 1 contoh soal:

Jake is 150 cm tall.Hobi is 160 cm tall.Hobi is taller than Jake and Jake is shorter than Hobi.

Mapel : Bahasa Inggris

Kelas : 6

Materi : Comperison forms

Kode Kelas : 5

Kode Kategorisasi : 6.5

Degrees of Comparison

Mana yang benar, "more tall than me" atau "more taller than me" atau "taller than me"?

Jawaban

Yang paling tepat di antara ketiga pilihan tersebut adalah taller than me.

___________

Penjelasan

Salah satu aturan dalam mengubah kata sifat menjadi bentuk comparative-nya yaitu:

Tambahkan imbuhan -er pada kata sifat yang hanya berisi satu suku kata.

Pilihan "more taller than" jelas-jelas keliru karena bentuk comparative-nya  diulang-ulang sehingga menjadi berlebihan.

Pilihan "more tall" kurang tepat karena tidak sesuai dengan aturan grammar dan kelihatannya tidak biasa bagi penutur asli Bahasa Inggris.

37. Writing assignment about "Endangered Species" More than 200 words


Jawaban:

Orangutan

The orangutans (also spelled orang-utan, orangutang, or orang-utang) are three extant species of great apes native to Indonesia and Malaysia. Orangutans are currently found in the rainforests of Borneo and Sumatra, but during the Pleistocene they ranged throughout Southeast Asia and South China. Classified in the genus Pongo, orangutans were originally considered to be one species. From 1996, they were divided into two species: the Bornean orangutan (P. pygmaeus, with three subspecies) and the Sumatran orangutan (P. abelii). In November 2017, it was reported that a third species had been identified: the Tapanuli orangutan (P. tapanuliensis).

Orangutans are among the most intelligent primates; they use a variety of sophisticated tools and construct elaborate sleeping nests each night from branches and foliage. The apes have been extensively studied for their learning abilities. There may even be distinctive cultures within populations. Field studies of the apes were pioneered by primatologist Birutė Galdikas. All three orangutan species are considered to be critically endangered. Human activities have caused severe declines in populations and ranges. Threats to wild orangutan populations include poaching, habitat destruction as a result of palm oil cultivation, and the illegal pet trade. Several conservation and rehabilitation organisations are dedicated to the survival of orangutans in the wild.
