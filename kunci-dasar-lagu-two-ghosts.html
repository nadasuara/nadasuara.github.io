23. (a) The lonely man in the corner quietly drank a pint of beer in the pub. (b) I do not understand why ghosts cannot be seen in the dark. Can someone tell me which is the noun phrase of this sentence? thanks in advance of you helped


NOUN PHRASE

(a) The lonely man in the corner  

a pint of beer

the pub

(b) the dark

------------------

EXPLANATION

Noun phrase adalah sebuah frasa benda, sekelompok dua atau lebih kata dengan sebuah kata benda sebagai induknya (head).

Sebuah noun phrase terdiri dari kata benda dan modifier yang menjelaskan kata benda tersebut.

Modifiernya dapat berupa article, determiner, adjective, dan prepositional phrase.

Noun phrase berfungsi sebagai:

subject ➛ The pretty girl gives the homeless man some money.object langsung ➛ The pretty girl gives the homeless man some money.object tidak langsung ➛ The pretty girl gives the homeless man some money.object of preposition ➛ The pretty girl takes off her shoes.appositive ➛ Sally, the pretty girl, gives the homeless man some money.subject complement ➛ Sally is a pretty girl.object complement ➛ They voted Sally the prettiest girl in class.adverbial ➛ Sally gave the man money last night.determinative ➛ Sally is wearing her boyfriend's jacket.

---------------

EXPLICATION

The lonely man in the corner quietly drank a pint of beer in the pub.

Subject = The lonely man in the corner = noun phrase karena terdiri dari lebih dari satu kata

Object = a pint of beer = noun phrase

object of the preposition 'in' = the pub = noun phrase

I do not understand why ghosts cannot be seen in the dark.

Subject = I = not a noun phrase karena hanya terdiri dari 1 kata

Object = why ghosts cannot be seen in the dark = clause karena mempunyai finite verb; bukan noun phrase

object of the preposition 'in' = the dark = noun phrase
